InstaFilter

iOS App \ Product Design 2025

InstaFilter is a filter discovery app designed for social media users.Leveraging crowd wisdom and algorithms, it helps users find their perfect style in a sea of filters.

Achievements

  • Global Downloads 650,000+
  • #1 App Store Free Apps (Taiwan, Jan 2025)
  • #1 Utilities (Taiwan, Hong Kong, Thailand)

Design Concept

Uncovering the pain point that "users can't find the filters they want," we combined service design methods to define MVP features and launched quickly, iterating based on user feedback.
