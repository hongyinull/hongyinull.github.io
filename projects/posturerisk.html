PostureRisk

Ergonomics \ Computer Vision 2025

PostureRisk is a posture risk assessment app designed to make "posture assessment accessible" and "adoptable in the field".

Core Technology

Using iPhone skeleton tracking to obtain joint geometry, implementing REBA (Rapid Entire Body Assessment) algorithms to generate portable, real-time risk quantification and reports.
